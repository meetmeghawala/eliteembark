Annapurna Base Camp

The Annapurna Base Camp Trek is one of the most popular trekking routes in Nepal, offering stunning views of the Annapurna massif and diverse landscapes. This trek takes you through charming Gurung villages, lush rhododendron forests, and across terraced fields, culminating in the magnificent view of the Annapurna peaks from base camp.
